About Masaba Charity Uganda

we are committed to helping all children who are helpless

Masaba Charity Uganda is a non political, nonprofit making organization. We consider diversity, inclusiveness, gender equality and general societal principle of natural Justice and Equity. Our major target is to reach out to street children, minority groups, the disabled, girls and women. We also take pride in participating in environmental advocacy and land restoration. We have programs that target Education, Talent development, shelter, medication, children rights advocacy. We also engage in community outreaches, environment activities in tree planting especially for disadvantaged communities such as landslide prone/flood prone areas. Masaba Charity Uganda started in 2014 but was formally registered with the government of Uganda in 2019.
